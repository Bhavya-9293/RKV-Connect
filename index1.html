SRC - Hackathon

SRC is hosting a 12-hour CSE Hackathon on feb 26th from 8 AM to 8 PM at the CSE Department. Teams of four will receive a problem statement on the day and can use any tech stack to develop their solution.

Career Guidance Meet along with Q&A Session

The SRC and RGU RAA are organizing a Career Guidance Meet and Q&A session on feb 28th from 9:30 AM to 12:30 PM in the Big Seminar Hall. The event will feature Bhanu Prasad Derangula and G Srinivasulu, both software engineers, who will share their experiences and provide career advice.

Career Guidance Meet along with Q&A Session

The RGUKT Alumni Association is hosting a Career Guidance Meet with a Q&A session on March 1st at 10:30 AM in F12, ECE Department, RGUKT, RK Valley. Guest speakers include Vyshnavi Vangari, a Senior Engineer at Samsung Semiconductor India, and Ramu Basavaraju, a PHY Algorithms Engineer at Tejas Networks.
